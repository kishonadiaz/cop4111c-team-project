Rock! Paper! Scissors!

Rules
Rules Of the Game

The game is played where players deliver hand signals that will represent the elements of the game; rock, paper and scissors. The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules:

  • Rock wins against scissors.
  • Scissors win against paper.
  • Paper wins against rock.

Click on the 'New Game' button to begin a new game and then object of your choosing (Rock, Paper or Scissors to start).

Click on the 'Resume Game' button to load a game/resume a game you were previously playing.

Click on the 'LeaderBoard' button to view the highscores and to see who is in the lead.
